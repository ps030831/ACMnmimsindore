About Us

The ACM Chapter at SVKM's NMIMS Indore is a student-driven community fostering innovation and excellence in technology. We host workshops, hackathons, and competitions to empower students in areas like AI, data science, and software engineering. Our mission is to inspire creativity, collaboration, and impactful tech solutions.
